Character Overview

Deep dive into the lore and capabilities of Castorice. A scholar associated with the Memokeepers, she weaves the threads of memory and reality.

Affiliation

Memokeepers / Garden of Recollection

Combat Type

Quantum

Path

The Erudition (Speculated)

Lore

Castorice is known to interact with the Memokeepers, entities who give up their physical bodies to travel the universe as data. Her connection to "Death" suggests a deeper, perhaps darker, understanding of existence.

Abilities

Specializes in manipulating memories and dealing AoE damage. Her ultimate is rumored to involve summoning "Netherwing", a memosprite construct.
