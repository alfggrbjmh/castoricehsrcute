About Castorice

Concept & Design

Castorice stands out in the Honkai: Star Rail universe with her distinct gothic lolita aesthetic combined with ethereal, almost ghostly elements. Her design reflects her connection to the 'Garden of Recollection', balancing beauty and an unsettling aura of death and memory loss.

The prominent use of lavender, skeletal motifs, and butterflies symbolizes transformation and the fleeting nature of life, which aligns perfectly with her lore as a Memokeeper.

"Memories are like petals in the wind... beautiful, fragile, and destined to scatter."

Role in Story

While much of her story is shrouded in mystery, Castorice plays a pivotal role in the deciding which truths are worth saving and which should be allowed to fade into oblivion.
